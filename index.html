Law Abiding Worker

This is a blog by a worker frustrated with the system rigged against them for the benefit of the people at the top (i.e. the ruling class).

The vast majority of workers (like the author of this blog) have been following the rules set out by the system (i.e. law-abiding), but they still get squeezed harder and harder for the benefits of the ruling class.

Many workers are feeling the same. This blog is aimed at sharing ideas, opinions, and observations on this topic.
